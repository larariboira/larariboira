“Make The Planet Greta Again” is an online entertainment video game developed on the proposal of the “Gamers4nature” team, to promote awareness of the existence of microplastics and their impact on the environment. In this way, our game intends to raise awareness of the problem of ocean contamination through plastic waste and facilitate the internalization of its negative impact on marine ecosystems, with the intervention of Greta Thunberg, the main character of the game.
